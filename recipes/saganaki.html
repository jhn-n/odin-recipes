Saganaki Recipe

Description

Saganaki is a popular Greek appetizer of pan-seared cheese, often served in a small, two-handled frying pan.

This is a simple recipe that makes a tasty starter.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Olive oil
  • Cheese (obviously)

Steps

  1. Cover the surface of the cheese with cold water
  2. Dredge the cheese in flour
  3. Pan fry with olive oil
  4. Don't forget to flip!
