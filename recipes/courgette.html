Saganaki Recipe

Description

Courgette balls, or kolokithokeftedes, are delicious fried pancakes or fritters with grated courgettes, crumbled feta cheese and fresh mint.

This is a simple recipe that makes a tasty side.

Ingredients

  • Crumbled Greek feta cheese
  • Eggs
  • Courgettes (obvs)
  • ...

Steps

  1. Grate and drain the courgettes
  2. Prepare the batter
  3. Deep fry
  4. Enjoy!
